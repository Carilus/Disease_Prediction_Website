Disease Prediction System - V1.0


This project is based on the predictive ability of Machine Learning. It is capable of predicing a disease from its symptoms. A user is required to populate the symptoms he/she is experiencing from the provided select boxes. It takes about 10 to 20 secomds to produce the most possible disease. After the predctions, a user may connect to the nearest doctor. The system stores every prediction outcome for future reference. Furthermore, the system recommends the various drugs approved by the Center of Disease Control and the World Health Organization for treatment of the predicted disease.
