Drug Recommendation

The system recommends a user to the various drugs that are approved by the Center of Disease Control and the World Health Organization for treatment of the predicted disease.

Predictions

Based on the symptoms populated by a user, the system builds a Support Vector Machine Classifier Model to predict the corresponding disease that the user is most probably suffering from. Thereafter the system displays the description and precautions of the predicted disease.

Linking Users to Doctors

After predictions, a user is may chose to contact a doctor for further treatment arrangements. The communication is initiated by the system through emails and then proceeeds off the system.
